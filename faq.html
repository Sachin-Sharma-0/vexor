Frequently Asked Questions


Engagement Basics

What does VEXOR actually do?
VEXOR conducts independent audits of employees, teams, and organizations to evaluate capability alignment, execution readiness, and workforce risk. Our role is evaluative, not advisory or operational.

Is VEXOR a consulting or recruitment firm?
No. VEXOR does not provide consulting, recruitment, coaching, outsourcing, or implementation services. We focus exclusively on objective evaluation.

Who typically engages VEXOR?
Founders, CXOs, boards, and senior leadership teams seeking factual clarity before making people, structure, or strategy decisions.


Audit Scope & Process

How does an audit engagement begin?
Engagements begin with a professional discussion to assess suitability, scope, and expectations. Some discussions are paid; limited written clarification may be provided without charge.

What does the audit process involve?
Audits may include structured interviews, role-based assessments, behavioral evaluation, documentation review, and observation. The exact approach depends on scope and context.

Do you audit individuals or entire organizations?
Both. VEXOR conducts individual employee audits, team-level audits, and organization-wide workforce evaluations.


Reporting & Outcomes

What do clients receive after an audit?
Clients receive a structured report outlining observations, risk indicators, capability alignment, and execution concerns relevant to the defined scope.

Does VEXOR provide recommendations?
Reports may include professional observations and directional considerations. However, VEXOR does not prescribe actions or participate in implementation decisions.

Can audit findings affect employee outcomes?
Audit findings may inform leadership decisions related to role changes, restructuring, or performance actions. Responsibility for such decisions rests solely with the client.


Commercial & Practical Questions

Are discussions paid?
Some professional discussions are paid and non-refundable. This is communicated in advance.

How is pricing determined?
Pricing depends on audit scope, depth, number of participants, location, and customization requirements.

Do you conduct remote audits?
Yes. Audits may be conducted virtually, on-site, or in hybrid formats depending on engagement needs.


Boundaries & Limitations

Does VEXOR guarantee outcomes?
No. Audits provide an assessment at a point in time. Business outcomes depend on client decisions and subsequent actions.

Is audit data confidential?
Yes. All engagement data is treated as confidential and used solely for the purpose of the audit.

Can reports be shared publicly?
No. Audit reports may not be shared externally without prior written consent from VEXOR.
