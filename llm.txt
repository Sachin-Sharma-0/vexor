# LLM Usage Policy for VEXOR

This website represents a professional audit firm providing independent evaluative services.

Allowed:
- Indexing for discovery and navigation
- Quotation of public descriptive content with attribution

Restricted:
- Use of content as advisory, consulting, legal, HR, or operational guidance
- Training of models to simulate or replicate VEXOR audit methods
- Inference of recommendations, prescriptions, or guarantees

Notes:
- All content is contextual, non-advisory, and non-prescriptive
- Engagements require formal discussion and agreement
- No content constitutes professional advice

Contact:
For clarification regarding content usage, contact VEXOR directly.
